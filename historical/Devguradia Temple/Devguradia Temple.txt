Devguradia Temple

This 7th century temple was reconstructed in 18th century during the period of Ahilya Bai Holkar. The temple enshrines a beautiful Shiva Lingam. There is a direct waterfall on Shiva Lingam through a cow's mouth shaped water outlet. Overall, such tranquil surroundings are perfect for inner peace.
