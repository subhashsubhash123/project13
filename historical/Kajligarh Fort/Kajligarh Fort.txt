Kajligarh Fort

Whether or not you are a history buff, attraction of Kajligarh Fort remains the same. This ruined old fort is basically a parkota that is surrounded with deep valley on three sides.  Indore city has many significant historical places or monuments associated with the great Holkar Dynastry.One such historical place in the outskirt of Indore City is the Kajligarh Fort, located about 20 km away from Indore City. The fort is nestled on a hill and is surrounded by a valley having a small waterfall.
