Shree Sansthan Bada Rawala

The oldest fort of Indore, Shri Sansthan Bada Rawla, is a historic structure dating back to the late seventeenth century. Popularly known as Bada Rawla, it belongs to the Mandloi Zamindar family, who were the indigenous landholders in the area before the arrival of the Marathas in Malwa.
