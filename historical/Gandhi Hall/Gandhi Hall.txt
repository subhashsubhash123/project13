Gandhi Hall

Gandhi Hall, Indore Overview. Gandhi Hall, formerly known as King Edward Hall, is a historic building located in the heart of Indore, Madhya Pradesh. Constructed in 1904, the hall was named in honour of King Edward VII. However, after India gained independence, it was renamed Gandhi Hall in memory of Mahatma Gandhi.
